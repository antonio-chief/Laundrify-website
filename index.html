Customer support

  • 24/7 Customer Support: Accessible through chat, phone, or email for any inquiries or issues. FAQs and troubleshooting guides available in-app.
  • Laundry Care Tips: Educational resources on fabric care, washing temperatures, and stain treatments. .
  • Loyalty Programs: Rewards for frequent users, such as discounts or free services.
  • Fabric Care Information: Detailed information on how to care for different fabrics. Washing symbols explained for better customer understanding..

Laundry services

  • Wash-and-Fold: Convenient drop-off service where clothes are washed, dried, and folded by professionals.
  • Dry Cleaning: Professional cleaning for delicate fabrics that cannot be washed with water. Special care for items like suits, dresses, and silk garments.
  • Specialty Cleaning: Treatment for items like comforters, blankets, and pillows. Services for sports uniforms, costumes, and other unique items.
  • Stain Removal Treatments: Pre-treatment for difficult stains (wine, grease, ink) before washing.

Self-service options

  • Reservation of Machines: Option to book machines in advance to avoid wait times. Notifications when reserved machines are available.
  • Laundry Supplies Availability: Vending machines or in-app purchasing options for detergent, fabric softener, and dryer sheets.
  • Access to Self-Service Washers and Dryers: Availability of various machine sizes for different loads.

Pickup and delivery

  • Real-Time Tracking: Live updates and tracking of pickup and delivery status through the app. Notifications when laundry is ready for pickup or delivery.
  • Scheduled Pickups: Users can select specific time slots for laundry pickup.
  • Timely Delivery: Guaranteed delivery times to ensure convenience. Alerts and notifications when laundry is on the way.

Services

What we do offer

Seamless convenience

Laundrify delivers unparalleled convenience by allowing users to schedule, track, and receive laundry services with just a few taps on our intuitive mobile app.
Our user-friendly interface ensures a seamless experience from order placement to doorstep delivery, eliminating the hassles of traditional laundry processes.

Real-time visibility

Laundrify keeps customers in the loop with real-time updates on their laundry status, from pickup to cleaning and delivery.
Through our advanced tracking system, users can monitor the progress of their laundry in real time, enhancing transparency and providing peace of mind.

Quality assurance

We ensure top-notch quality with rigorous quality control measures at every stage of the laundry process.
From the selection of trusted laundry facilities to thorough inspections before delivery, we prioritize quality to exceed customer expectations consistently.

Flexible service options

Laundrify caters to diverse customer needs with flexible service options, including on-demand and scheduled pickups, as well as customizable preferences.
Users can tailor their laundry preferences through our app, choosing specific detergents, wash temperatures, and folding preferences, providing a personalized experience.

Secure & contactless transactions

Laundrify prioritizes customer safety and convenience with secure and contactless payment options.
Our platform ensures the privacy and security of user data, and transactions can be completed without the need for physical contact, aligning with current health and safety preferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Our app allows you to easily order laundry services from local laundromat providers. Simply download the app, create an account, and browse available services in your area. You can schedule pickups and deliveries at your convenience, making laundry hassle-free!

Payment is simple and secure! You can pay directly through the app using various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and mpesa. Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a receipt via email for your records.

You can order a variety of laundry services, including wash-and-fold, dry cleaning, and specialty services like delicates or large items. Each service provider offers different options, so you can choose what best fits your needs.
